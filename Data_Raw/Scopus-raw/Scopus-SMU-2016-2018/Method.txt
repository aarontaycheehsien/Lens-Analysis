To get papers with SMU byline published between 2016 to 2018 in Scopus

A query was run in Scopus (www.Scopus.com) on 21th March 2019

1. Click on Affiliations 

2. Enter Singapore Management University & search

3. Limit Year facet to 2016, 2017 & 2018

4. Click on All - Select all 

5. Click Export -> CSV Export -> Export -> Scopus-2016-2018.csv
