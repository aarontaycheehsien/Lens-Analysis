To get papers with SMU byline published between 1999 to 2010 in Scopus

A query was run in Scopus (www.Scopus.com) on 21th March 2019

1. Click on Affiliations 

2. Enter Singapore Management University & search

3. Limit Year facet from 1999 to 2010

4. Click on All - Select all 

5. Click Export -> CSV Export -> Export -> Scopus-1999-2010.csv
